Refund Policy - Web Development and Custom Software Development Agency

1. Refund Eligibility
1.1 Web Development: Refunds may be eligible in the following circumstances:
a. If the project has not yet commenced and the client requests a cancellation.
b. If our agency fails to deliver the agreed-upon web development services within the specified timeline, subject to any agreed-upon extensions or force majeure events.
c. If the delivered website does not meet the agreed-upon specifications and cannot be resolved through revisions or fixes.

1.2 Custom Software Development: Refunds may be eligible in the following circumstances:
a. If the project has not yet commenced, and the client requests a cancellation.
b. If our agency fails to deliver the agreed-upon custom software development services within the specified timeline, subject to any agreed-upon extensions or force majeure events.
c. If critical functionality or features specified in the project requirements cannot be implemented and cannot be resolved through alternatives or workarounds.

2. Refund Process
2.1 Requesting a Refund: To request a refund, the client must notify our agency in writing, clearly stating the reasons for the refund request and providing any supporting documentation or evidence.
2.2 Evaluation and Resolution: Our agency will review the refund request and evaluate its eligibility based on the terms outlined in Section 1. If the refund is deemed eligible, we will work with the client to determine an appropriate resolution, which may include revising the project, providing partial refunds, or issuing a full refund.
2.3 Refund Amount: The refund amount, if approved, will be determined on a case-by-case basis, taking into consideration the work completed, project expenses, and any applicable cancellation fees. The refund may exclude any third-party costs or materials that have been purchased or used specifically for the project.
2.4 Refund Method: Refunds will be issued using the same method of payment originally used by the client. If that is not feasible, alternative arrangements will be made in consultation with the client.

3. Non-Refundable Items and Services
3.1 Domain Registrations and Hosting: Any fees paid for domain registrations and hosting services are non-refundable, as these services are provided by third-party providers.
3.2 Third-Party Services and Products: Our agency may use third-party services or products as part of the web development or custom software development project. Any fees or costs associated with these third-party services or products are non-refundable, as they are subject to the terms and conditions of the respective providers.

4. No Refunds for Completed Work
No refunds will be provided for work that has been completed and delivered to the client, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing. Revisions or fixes for completed work will be provided within the scope of the project, but refunds will not be issued for work that has already been finalized and accepted by the client.

Please note that the above refund policy is a general sample and may need to be customized to fit the specific requirements and legal considerations of your web development and custom software development agency. It is recommended to seek legal advice when creating or modifying refund policies for your business.
